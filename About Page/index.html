Menopause
Made Easy

At Nuher, our mission is simple — to make menopause less confusing and more empowering for every woman. We understand that the journey through perimenopause and menopause can be overwhelming, especially with so much misinformation and stigma around it. We’re here to offer clarity, care, and comfort through every stage of this transition.

Nuher is a platform dedicated to supporting women’s health during midlife. We evaluate co-existing health issues and we put all the pieces together and come up with a plan of treatment. We  prioritize what you feel. Be it  getting rid of menopausal symptoms, getting a good night’s sleep , a better libido, relief from vaginal dryness or a better sex life. If you’re focused on losing weight or  improving your hair and skin. At NuHer, we address all your concerns relating to hormones along with  your non-hormonal issues.

Our approach combines expert medical guidance with science-backed treatments, helping women manage symptoms of menopause with confidence. We provide doctor-prescribed, hormone replacement therapy (HRT) that is customized to your unique needs — and conveniently delivered right to your doorstep. We take a personalized, holistic, and long-term approach to menopause care — focusing on improving your overall health, balance, and longevity. At Nuher, we’re not just treating menopause — we’re redefining how women experience it.

Why Menopause

140m+

By 2024, over 140 million women in India are likely to experience menopause, typically between ages 45 and 55.

50%

Nearly 50% of Indian women aged 35–49 are either overweight or obese, which can exacerbate menopausal symptoms.

81%

81% of women have one or more menopausal symptoms, yet only 48% seek treatment.
