of Co-founder

I entered menopause before I entered midlife. At 37, cancer treatment pushed my body into menopause overnight. I was prepared to fight for my life. I was not prepared for the silence — or how little clear, science-based menopause support existed.

I spoke openly about cancer stigma. But menopause? I carried that alone. Like many women, I thought the exhaustion, brain fog, and anxiety were simply something to endure.

‘’ NuHer was born from that realisation — and from honest conversations with women living the same experience. We are building NuHer to change that. To create a safe space where women are seen, heard, and supported with both science and real understanding. Because menopause is not a medical dead end. And suffering is not the price of ageing. Menopause is not the beginning of the end. It is a woman’s Primetime.’’

Lisa Ray, Co-Founder

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is menopause and when does it typically occur in India?

⌄

Menopause marks the end of a woman’s menstrual cycle, confirmed after 12 months without periods. In India, the average age of natural menopause is around 46–48 years, though some women may experience it earlier or later.

What are common symptoms of menopause?

⌄

Common symptoms include hot flashes, night sweats, mood changes, sleep disturbances, and hormonal imbalance.

What is Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)?

⌄

HRT is a treatment used to relieve symptoms of menopause by replacing hormones that the body no longer produces.

Is HRT safe for Indian women?

⌄

HRT can be safe when prescribed and monitored by a qualified healthcare provider based on individual needs.
